Loudspeaker Primer

The acoustical amplification of sound has been wielded by humans since antiquity, and with the advent of electrically induced sound reproduction in the midst of the industrial revolution, the whole process of saving sounds and playing them back became a complicated new art-science. From infinite baffle, to direct radiating, to electrostatic panels and even controlled arcs of plasma, there are so many interesting ways to make and remake sound. I tend to favor older designs that rely on the natural laws of acoustics to recreate sound rather than using computational digital magic. Below I picked the three designs that I've known about the longest.

  • Direct Radiating
  • Horn Loaded
  • Electrostatic Panel
